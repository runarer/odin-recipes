Pancakes

Description

Thin nice pancakes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted, or as needed

How to:

  1. Blend wet stuff
  2. Blend dry stuff
  3. Mix both
  4. Cook in a fryingpan.
